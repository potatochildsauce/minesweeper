How to play

The rules of the field
Reveal squares
Click any tile to reveal it. If it shows a number, that's how many mines hide in the surrounding squares. A blank tile means no mines nearby — it auto-opens its neighbors. The first click is always safe.
Flag suspects
Right-click (or long-press on mobile) to plant a flag where you think a mine is. Flagged tiles can't be revealed accidentally. The counter shows mines remaining.
Chord-click for speed
Click a revealed number when its flags match its value — all remaining neighbors open at once. A wrong flag here can end the game.
Keyboard play
Arrows to move. Space or Enter to reveal. F to flag. R for a new game. Esc closes dialogs.
Stuck? Use a Hint
Each board gives you one hint. Press the Hint button or H and a guaranteed-safe tile will pulse. Hints reduce your score, so save them for when you really need one.
Share boards
After you win, copy your board code and send it to a friend. Or paste their code with the Import button below to play the exact same field.
